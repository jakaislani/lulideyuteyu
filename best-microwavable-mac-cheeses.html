We Rank the Best Microwavable Mac and Cheese

July 06, 2024
blog

The best microwave mac and cheese tastes like stovetop mac and cheese. And like a good friend, microwave mac and cheese is always there for you. Plus, it’s quick, reliable, and easy. You just need the correct household appliance and you can have a warm, comforting meal ready in minutes. When I was growing up, microwavable mac and cheese was a tasty, convenient snack and I always loved it. As an adult, however, my food standards are higher and it doesn’t always hold up.

Most of the microwavable macs we tried were bad (like, very, very bad), but we found an elite few that are worthy of gracing your grocery cart. The microwavable macaroni and cheeses on our list are creamy, affordable, and, above all else, quick!

Before I get into that, I do want to address that most microwave mac and cheese has instructions that I think are wrong. If you’ve ever had a watery cup or instant mac and cheese, follow these rules that I made up that are correct. Deal with it, Kraft!

  • Make sure to only microwave one cup at a time. This isn’t listed on the container, but it is important for cooking. If you try to cook two at once, you’re more likely to mess up the texture of the noodles.
  • Add in the powder ahead of time. All of these have you add the powder after cooking and I do not understand why. If you add the powder with the water before cooking, it makes the sauce so creamy and thick.
  • Drain the water. If you don’t want to add the cheese powder prior to cooking, drain the noodles! It explicitly tells you not to, but that is wrong. You don’t have to drain out all the liquid, but at least drain some. If you don’t, some of these are way too watery.

    Best as a Side

    Bob Evans Tasteful Sides Macaroni & Cheese

    It is so funny to me that they call this tub of microwavable macaroni and cheese “tasteful.” That being said, it is tasty! Bob Evans’s mac tastes like a better version of KFC’s mac and cheese. It shines when it comes to sodium—it’s so scrumptiously salty! Justine Sterling, editor-in-chief of Sporked, loved the “extreme cheese coverage” that was luscious without being overwhelming. Slap this in a bowl and serve it at Thanksgiving. No one will know you didn’t make it from scratch.

    Most Unique

    Panera Broccoli Cheddar Mac & CheeseTarget

    Toss the regular Panera mac and cheese out the window. This broccoli cheddar rendition is so much better. It’s exactly like Panera’s broccoli cheddar soup mixed with Panera’s mac and cheese, except you don’t have to leave your house or buy and mix two different items together. This is creamy, a little tangy, and rich. I don’t think I’ll ever go to a Panera again. I have all I need in this microwave mac and cheese.

    Best Splurge

    Cracker Barrel Sharp Cheddar Macaroni & Cheese Dinner

    Cracker Barrel’s microwavable mac and cheese dinner, with its luxe gold and black packaging, is the Lamborghini of microwaveable mac and cheese. It comes with multiple flavor packets and a little bag of breadcrumbs, which our team had conflicting feelings about. Some of us thought they tasted a little raw, while others thought they added a great crunch. But we could all agree on one thing: This mac and cheese is creamy, sumptuous, and delicious. It is certainly one of the best microwave mac and cheese brands we tried.

    Best of the Best

    Annie’s Rich & Creamy Classic CheddarTarget

    I cannot believe that I am giving an Annie’s product the #1 spot on a ranking. They have an extremely low batting average for us here at Sporked. That being said, we have no real brand loyalty to anyone. If something is good, we’ll tell you about it. And this microwaveable mac and cheese isn’t just good. It’s great. It tastes like you made it on the stovetop. It’s so creamy. The shells were perfectly al dente. This is a perfect 10 and definitely the best microwaveable mac and cheese we tasted.
