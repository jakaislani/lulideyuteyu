About Consulate General of Ukraine in New York, USA

Ukrainian Consulate in New York runs an inclusive range of consular services to local, Ukrainian, and international citizens in United-states.

The Ukrainian Consulate in New York supports American citizens through its consular services, if they:

  • Need general or specific info regarding Ukrainian economy, culture, sports, education and more
  • Need information regarding requirements and process of getting Ukrainian Citizenship
  • Specific contacts and information in Ukraine
  • Want to apply and obtain Ukrainian Visa

Visa and Passport services

Ukrainian passport and visa from are issued only to citizens whose main residence is in Ukraine and United-states.

The process of issuing Ukrainian Visa and Passport of Ukraine can take few weeks.

*Please contact with Ukrainian Consulate in New York if you have any question as regards of visa and passport matters.

Ukrainian representations in United-states

In addition to the Ukrainian Consulate in New York, Ukraine also has 3 other representations in United-states:

  • Ukrainian Consulate in San Francisco
  • Ukrainian Consulate in Chicago
  • Ukrainian Embassy in Washington DC

American representations in Ukraine

  • American Embassy in Kiev is the only American representation in Ukraine

Ukrainian representations worldwide

Ukrainian Consulate In New York represents one of 123 consular and diplomatic representations of Ukraine all over the world.

Foreign representations in United-states

Ukrainian Consulate In New York represents one of 922 foreign consular and diplomatic representations from around the world in United-states.
