Who is Paul Walkers twin?

May 22, 2024
blog

Before his untimely death in a car accident in 2013 at the age of 40, American actor and philanthropist Paul Walker starred in the quick and furious franchise.

His brothers, the twins Cody and Caleb, were able to reprise his role and finish filming Fast and Furious 7 after he passed away, thus his legacy lives on.

Walker started acting when he was young, but he first got prominence for his role on the soap opera The Young and the Restless in the 1990s.

After that, he appeared in movies including 1999’s Varsity Blues.

Who is Paul Walker’s twin?

Paul Walker is not a twin as speculations have it. However, he has twin brothers by the names of Cody and Caleb Walker.

Cody Beau Walker, the younger brother of Paul Walker is often referred to as his twins after he and his twin brother Caleb assisted in finishing Brian O’Conner’s final sequences in the 2015 movie Furious 7 when Paul Walker passed on.
