Bikini-Clad Taraji P. Henson Enjoys Yacht Party to Celebrate 50th Birthday in Mexico

August 17, 2024
blog

American actress, Taraji P. Henson, turned 50 on Friday, September 11, and opted for a lavish celebration in Mexico, joined by her friends Mary J. Blige, Angie Martinez, and others. 

She took to Instagram to share a series of photos wearing a white two-piece bikini, while on a yacht, as she marked her special day.  

“Go off shawty it’s ya BIRFDAY,” she captioned the photos, referencing 50 Cent‘s 2003 song “In Da Club.”
